Speech Analysis for Diagnosis of Parkinson's

Developed an innovative algorithm using machine learning to diagnose Parkinson's disease based on voice analysis. Utilized genetic algorithms to optimize feature selection and support vector machines for classification, achieving an accuracy of 92% and sensitivity of 100%. This groundbreaking approach was published in Materials Today -- Proceedings by Elsevier Ltd.

Overview:

Developed a new algorithm for diagnosing Parkinson's disease based on voice analysis.

Technologies:

Machine Learning, MATLAB, Computer Networks

Methodology:

  • Employed a genetic algorithm (GA) to select 32 optimized features from all extracted features.
  • Utilized a support vector machine (SVM) to classify the data as normal or abnormal based on validation size.

Outcome:

Achieved an accuracy of 92% and a sensitivity of 100%.

Publication:

Published a research paper on this algorithm in Materials Today -- Proceedings, published by Elsevier Ltd.
